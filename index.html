Vision

Saving Our Planet Through the Power of Plants

The vision of Koman Keneya is to create a better future through the power of medicinal plants and to help people improve themselves with ancestral knowledge of medicine. We are increasingly losing the culture of our ancestors.

The school seeks to foster innovation, strengthen the global presence of plants, and better connect with communities — inviting interested people from around the world to experience real healing opportunities within the Shipibo tradition.

The Maestro

A Healer Born Under the Shihuahuaco Tree

Originally from the Roaboya Nativa community near Pucallpa, Peru, Americo López Sanchez is a dedicated Shipibo curandero (healer) whose traditional name translates to “the many colors of nature.” Inspired by the lineage of his father, a prominent healer, Americo overcame a challenging childhood to pursue his calling. His formal training began at age 15, leading to years of rigorous, isolated diets with Master Plants, Ayahuasca ceremonies, and deep spiritual trials. Today, he channels this profound resilience into helping those who are suffering, lost, or seeking a genuine cure.

As a former school teacher and eloquent storyteller, Americo blends his healing practice with a passion for education and community service. He works extensively with youth to preserve Shipibo culture and pass down his vast knowledge of medicinal plants. Driven by a lifelong commitment to growth, his mission is to revive ancestral education methods—using traditional diets and sacred songs—to help the natural gifts of the next generation shine.

The Experience

What Your Retreat Includes

Every stay at Koman Keneya is a full immersion — combining ceremony, rest, nourishment, and cultural transmission into one coherent, tradition-guided experience.

01
Plant Ceremonies
Ayahuasca & Master Plants

Healing ceremonies with sacred icaros in a traditional maloca, guided by Maestro Americo.

02
Private Tambo Lodging
Rest & Integration

Your own handcrafted wooden tambo with hammock porch, comfortable bedding, and views of the Amazonian landscape.

03
Medicinal Baths & Saunas
Purification Rituals

Traditional Shipibo purification through saunas, aromatic plant baths, and emetics — preparing the body and spirit for deeper healing.

04
Traditional Cuisine
Dieta-Aligned Meals

All meals prepared by our resident chef following traditional dieta guidelines — simple, clean food that supports your healing and plant work.

05
Education
Living Classroom

Walk into the Amazon with the Maestro to learn plant identification, traditional uses, and the deep stories encoded in the forest.

06
Shipibo Arts & Culture
Embroidery, Song & Dance

Learn the visual language of the Shipibo through traditional embroidery with geometric designs, ancestral songs, cultural dances, and games.
