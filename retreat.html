Before You Arrive

What to Know About Your Retreat

Every retreat at Koman Keneya is a full immersion into Shipibo tradition, held in the living Amazon with care, ceremony, and ancestral wisdom.

✦
Dieta Preparation

You will receive guidelines to begin your dieta before arrival: a clean diet that opens the body to the plants.

Private Tambo

Your own handcrafted wooden tambo with hammock porch and views of the Amazonian landscape. A space to rest and integrate.

Ceremony Space

Ceremonies are held in a traditional maloca, guided by Maestro Americo with sacred icaros and the medicine of the forest.

Traditional Cuisine

All meals follow traditional dieta guidelines: simple, clean, and prepared to support your healing and plant work.

Getting There

Fly into Pucallpa, Peru. Airport transport to and from the center is included in both retreat packages.

Small Group

Both retreats are intentionally kept small so that Maestro Americo can offer close guidance and personal attention to each participant.
